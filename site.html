Tic-Tac-Toe

We are providing you the Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe Game.
Rules for Tic-Tac-Toe :

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid
  • Players take turns marking empty spaces with an X or O
  • The first player to get three marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) wins
  • If all nine spaces are filled and no player has three marks in a row, the game ends in a tie or Draw
