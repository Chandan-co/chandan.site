Rules for TIC-TAC-TOE:

  1. The game is played on a 3x3 grid

  2. Players take turns marking empty spaces with an X or O

  3. The first player to get three marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) wins

  4. If all nine spaces are filled and no player has three marks in a row, the game ends in a tie or Draw
